‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new task?

Tap the floating "+" button on the Today, Calendar, or Matrix screens. Fill in the task details including title, due date, priority, and tags. You can also add subtasks and set custom reminders.

How does task synchronization work?

PlandoPro uses Firebase/Firestore to sync your tasks across all your devices in real-time. Simply sign in with your Google account, and your tasks will be automatically synchronized. The app also works offline, and changes will sync when you're back online.

What are recurring tasks?

Recurring tasks automatically create new instances based on your schedule (daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly). When you complete a recurring task, a new one is automatically generated for the next occurrence.

How do I use the Eisenhower Matrix?

Go to the Matrix tab and drag tasks into one of four quadrants: Urgent & Important, Not Urgent but Important, Urgent but Not Important, or Neither. This helps you prioritize what truly matters.

What is the Pomodoro Timer?

The Pomodoro technique helps you focus by working in 25-minute intervals with short breaks. Go to the Pomodoro tab, optionally link a task, and start the timer. You can customize work/break durations and enjoy ambient sounds while focusing.

How do I manage notifications?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Notifications to configure your preferences. You can enable/disable task reminders, daily summary notifications, and customize the daily review time. Make sure notifications are enabled in iOS Settings as well.

What are the premium features?

Premium subscribers get access to unlimited tasks, advanced statistics, custom themes, priority support, and future premium features. You can subscribe through the Settings page.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Subscription ‚Üí Restore Purchases. This will restore any active subscriptions associated with your Apple ID.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management to export your tasks. You can also request a complete data export by contacting our support team.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data from our servers. This action cannot be undone, so please make sure to export your data first if needed.

üîß Troubleshooting

Tasks not syncing?

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Make sure you're signed in with your Google account
  3. Try signing out and signing back in
  4. Restart the app

Notifications not working?

  1. Go to iOS Settings ‚Üí PlandoPro ‚Üí Notifications and ensure they're enabled
  2. Check that Do Not Disturb is not active
  3. Verify notification settings within the PlandoPro app
  4. Try deleting and recreating the reminder

App crashing or freezing?

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of PlandoPro from the App Store
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for iOS updates
  4. If the problem persists, contact support with details about when the crash occurs

üí° Tips & Tricks

  • Quick Complete: Swipe right on any task to quickly mark it as complete
  • Quick Delete: Swipe left on any task to delete it
  • Undo: After completing or deleting a task, tap the undo button that appears at the bottom
  • Widget: Add the PlandoPro widget to your home screen for quick access to today's tasks
  • Deep Links: Use the widget's "New Task" button to quickly create tasks
  • Search: Use the search bar in the Today view to quickly find tasks
  • Batch Operations: Long-press on tasks for additional options

üîí Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. We never share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. All your task data is encrypted and stored securely in Firebase. You have full control over your data and can delete your account at any time. Read our full Privacy Policy for more details.
