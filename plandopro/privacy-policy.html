Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to PlandoPro! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our task management application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your name and profile picture (if provided by Google)
  • A unique user identifier from Google Authentication

1.2 Task and Usage Data

To provide our task management services, we store:

  • Tasks you create (title, description, due dates, tags, priority)
  • Subtasks and task completion status
  • Pomodoro timer records and focus duration
  • Reminder and notification preferences
  • App settings and customization preferences

1.3 Subscription Information

For premium features, we process subscription data through RevenueCat, including:

  • Subscription status and expiration dates
  • Purchase history (managed by Apple and RevenueCat)

1.4 Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and crash reports (for debugging purposes)
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve app performance

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the PlandoPro service
  • Sync your tasks across your devices via Firebase/Firestore
  • Send you task reminders and notifications (with your permission)
  • Manage your subscription status and premium features
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Firebase/Firestore: All task data is stored in Google's secure cloud infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest
  • Local Storage: Some data is cached locally on your device for offline access
  • Access Control: Your data is isolated per user account and only accessible by you

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We only share data with the following service providers necessary for app functionality:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and data synchronization
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (they do not access your task data)
  • Apple: For push notifications and in-app purchases

These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and only process data as instructed by us.

5. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app at any time
  • Modification: Edit or update your tasks and settings freely
  • Deletion: Delete individual tasks or your entire account at any time through the Settings page
  • Export: Request a copy of your data (contact us at the email below)
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and analytics in app settings

6. Data Retention

  • Active account data is retained as long as your account exists
  • When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup copies are removed from our systems within 90 days

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of PlandoPro after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

Email: sumect@gmail.com

隐私政策

最后更新：2025年1月

欢迎使用 PlandoPro！我们致力于保护您的隐私并确保您个人信息的安全。本隐私政策说明了当您使用我们的任务管理应用时，我们如何收集、使用和保护您的数据。

1. 我们收集的信息

1.1 账户信息

当您使用 Google 登录时，我们会收集：

  • 您的电子邮件地址
  • 您的姓名和头像（如果 Google 提供）
  • 来自 Google 身份验证的唯一用户标识符

1.2 任务和使用数据

为了提供任务管理服务，我们存储：

  • 您创建的任务（标题、描述、截止日期、标签、优先级）
  • 子任务和任务完成状态
  • 番茄钟计时记录和专注时长
  • 提醒和通知偏好设置
  • 应用设置和个性化偏好

1.3 订阅信息

对于高级功能，我们通过 RevenueCat 处理订阅数据，包括：

  • 订阅状态和到期日期
  • 购买历史记录（由 Apple 和 RevenueCat 管理）

1.4 技术信息

  • 设备类型和操作系统版本
  • 应用版本和崩溃报告（用于调试）
  • 匿名使用分析数据以改进应用性能

2. 我们如何使用您的信息

我们仅将您的信息用于：

  • 提供和维护 PlandoPro 服务
  • 通过 Firebase/Firestore 在您的设备间同步任务
  • 向您发送任务提醒和通知（需要您的许可）
  • 管理您的订阅状态和高级功能
  • 改进应用功能和用户体验
  • 提供客户支持
  • 确保应用安全并防止欺诈

3. 数据存储和安全

您的数据使用行业标准实践安全存储：

  • Firebase/Firestore：所有任务数据存储在 Google 的安全云基础设施中，传输和静态数据均加密
  • 本地存储：部分数据在您的设备上本地缓存以供离线访问
  • 访问控制：您的数据按用户账户隔离，仅您可以访问

4. 数据共享和第三方

我们不会出售、出租或与第三方共享您的个人信息用于营销目的。

我们仅与以下应用功能所必需的服务提供商共享数据：

  • Google Firebase：用于身份验证和数据同步
  • RevenueCat：用于订阅管理（他们无法访问您的任务数据）
  • Apple：用于推送通知和应用内购买

这些提供商受严格的保密协议约束，仅按我们的指示处理数据。

5. 您的权利和控制

您对自己的数据拥有完全控制权：

  • 访问：随时在应用内查看您的所有数据
  • 修改：自由编辑或更新您的任务和设置
  • 删除：随时通过设置页面删除单个任务或整个账户
  • 导出：请求获取您的数据副本（请通过下方邮箱联系我们）
  • 退出：在应用设置中禁用通知和分析

6. 数据保留

  • 只要您的账户存在，活跃账户数据就会被保留
  • 当您删除账户时，所有相关数据将在 30 天内永久删除
  • 备份副本将在 90 天内从我们的系统中删除

7. 政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。我们将通过应用或电子邮件通知您重大变更。在变更后继续使用 PlandoPro 即表示接受更新后的政策。
