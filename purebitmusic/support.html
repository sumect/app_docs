Getting Started

Welcome to PureBit Music! Here's how to get started with the app:

  1. Download and install PureBit Music from the App Store
  2. Create an account or sign in
  3. Add your local music files to the app
  4. Create playlists and enjoy your music
  5. Sync your playlists across your devices

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What audio formats does PureBit Music support?
A: PureBit Music supports common audio formats including MP3, AAC, FLAC, WAV, and more.
Q: How do I sync my playlists across devices?
A: Sign in with the same account on all your devices. Your playlists will automatically sync across all devices where you're signed in.
Q: Can I use PureBit Music offline?
A: Yes! Once you've added your local music files, you can play them offline anytime. Playlist syncing requires an internet connection.
Q: How do I delete my account?
A: You can request account deletion by contacting us at the email address below. We will process your request and delete all your data.
Q: Is my music data private?
A: Yes! We only collect playlist data and account information necessary for the service. We do not share your personal information with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
Q: How do I create a playlist?
A: Tap the "+" button in the Playlists section, give your playlist a name, and start adding songs from your library.

Troubleshooting

Music files not showing up

  • Make sure the files are in a supported audio format
  • Check that you've granted the app permission to access your files
  • Try refreshing your library

Playlists not syncing

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure you're signed in with the same account on all devices
  • Try signing out and signing back in

App crashes or freezes

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app
  • Try restarting the app
  • If the problem persists, contact us with details about when the crash occurs
