Getting Started

Creating Your First Supplement

To add a new supplement:

  • Tap the "+" button on the main screen
  • Enter the supplement name and dosage
  • Set your preferred schedule (daily, weekly, or custom)
  • Add inventory information (optional)
  • Save and you're done!

Setting Up Reminders

Never miss a dose with our reminder system:

  • Open any supplement from your list
  • Tap "Edit" and scroll to "Reminders"
  • Enable notifications and set your preferred times
  • Customize time labels (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, etc.)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I sync my data across devices?
A: Sign in with your Google or Apple account in Settings. Your data will automatically sync across all your devices.
Q: Can I use the app without an internet connection?
A: Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. You can use SuppleMate completely offline. Syncing only requires internet when you want to access data on multiple devices.
Q: How do I track my supplement inventory?
A: When editing a supplement, you can add the total quantity and set an expiration date. The app will automatically track remaining quantity as you log doses and alert you when running low.
Q: What's the difference between the free and premium versions?
A: The free version allows you to track unlimited supplements with basic features. Premium unlocks advanced analytics, custom time labels, cloud sync, and priority support.
Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will remove all your data from our servers. Note that local data will remain on your device until you uninstall the app.
Q: I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?
A: First, check that notifications are enabled in your device Settings > SuppleMate > Notifications. Also ensure that reminders are enabled for each supplement in the app.

Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  • Try restarting your device
  • If the problem persists, contact support with details about when the crash occurs

Sync Issues

  • Check your internet connection
  • Sign out and sign back in to your account
  • Ensure you're using the same account on all devices

Missing Data

  • If you're signed in, your data should sync automatically
  • Check if you're viewing the correct date range in history
  • Contact support if data is genuinely missing

常见问题

问:如何在设备之间同步数据?
答:在设置中使用您的 Google 或 Apple 账户登录。您的数据将自动在所有设备之间同步。
问:我可以在没有互联网连接的情况下使用应用吗?
答:可以!您的所有数据都存储在设备本地。您可以完全离线使用 SuppleMate。只有当您想在多个设备上访问数据时才需要互联网进行同步。
问:如何跟踪补充剂库存?
答:编辑补充剂时,您可以添加总数量并设置过期日期。当您记录剂量时,应用会自动跟踪剩余数量,并在库存不足时提醒您。
问:免费版和高级版有什么区别?
答:免费版允许您使用基本功能跟踪无限数量的补充剂。高级版解锁高级分析、自定义时间标签、云同步和优先支持。
问:如何删除我的账户?
答:前往 设置 > 账户 > 删除账户。这将从我们的服务器中删除您的所有数据。请注意,本地数据将保留在您的设备上,直到您卸载应用。
问:我没有收到通知。我该怎么办?
答:首先,检查设备设置 > SuppleMate > 通知中是否启用了通知。还要确保在应用中为每个补充剂启用了提醒。
