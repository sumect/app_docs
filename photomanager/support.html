Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are my photos stored on your servers?

A: No, absolutely not. All photo and video processing is performed entirely on your local device. Your media files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

Q: Why do I need to create an account?

A: The account is optional and is only used to share subscription authorization across other apps within our app suite. This allows you to access premium features across multiple apps with a single subscription.

Q: How can I delete my account?

A: You can delete your account at any time from the app settings. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Q: What happens to my subscription if I delete my account?

A: When you delete your account, your subscription status will be terminated. However, your photos and videos remain safe on your device as they were never uploaded to our servers.

Q: How do I report a bug or request a feature?

A: Please email us at sumect+photo@gmail.com with details about the bug or your feature request. We appreciate your feedback!

常见问题

问: 我的照片会存储在你们的服务器上吗?

答: 不会,绝对不会。所有照片和视频的处理完全在您的本地设备上完成。您的媒体文件永远不会离开您的设备,也不会上传到我们的服务器。

问: 为什么需要创建账户?

答: 账户是可选的,仅用于在我们应用套装内的其他应用之间共享订阅授权。这使您可以通过单个订阅访问多个应用的高级功能。

问: 如何删除我的账户?

答: 您可以随时从应用设置中删除您的账户。进入 设置 > 账户 > 删除账户。请注意,此操作是永久性的,无法撤销。

问: 如果我删除账户,我的订阅会怎样?

答: 当您删除账户时,您的订阅状态将被终止。但是,您的照片和视频仍然安全地保存在您的设备上,因为它们从未上传到我们的服务器。

问: 如何报告错误或请求新功能?

答: 请发送邮件至 sumect+photo@gmail.com,并详细说明错误或您的功能请求。我们非常感谢您的反馈!
