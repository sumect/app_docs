Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our photo management application.

Data Storage and Processing

We do not store any of your photos or videos. All photo and video processing is performed entirely on your local device. Your media files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party services.

Account Information

We provide an optional account login feature for the following purpose:

  • Subscription Sharing: Your account is used to share subscription authorization across other apps within our app suite. This allows you to access premium features across multiple apps with a single subscription.

When you create an account, we only collect the minimum information necessary for authentication and subscription management.

Account Deletion

You have full control over your account. You can delete your account at any time directly from the app settings. When you delete your account:

  • All your account information will be permanently removed from our servers
  • Your subscription status will be terminated
  • This action cannot be undone

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services only for subscription management and authentication. These services are carefully selected and comply with industry-standard privacy practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

隐私政策

最后更新时间：2025年1月

我们对您隐私的承诺

我们致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了当您使用我们的照片管理应用程序时,我们如何处理您的信息。

数据存储和处理

我们不存储您的任何照片或视频。所有照片和视频的处理完全在您的本地设备上完成。您的媒体文件永远不会离开您的设备,也不会上传到我们的服务器或任何第三方服务。

账户信息

我们提供可选的账户登录功能,用于以下目的:

  • 订阅共享:您的账户用于在我们应用套装内的其他应用之间共享订阅授权。这使您可以通过单个订阅访问多个应用的高级功能。

当您创建账户时,我们仅收集身份验证和订阅管理所需的最少信息。

账户删除

您对自己的账户拥有完全控制权。您可以随时直接从应用设置中删除您的账户。当您删除账户时:

  • 您的所有账户信息将从我们的服务器中永久删除
  • 您的订阅状态将被终止
  • 此操作无法撤销

第三方服务

我们仅将第三方服务用于订阅管理和身份验证。这些服务经过精心挑选,并符合行业标准的隐私实践。

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。我们将通过更新本政策顶部的"最后更新时间"来通知您任何变更。
